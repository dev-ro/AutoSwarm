# The Spiral Protocol: Expanded Scene List (40 Scenes)

This document provides a detailed 40-scene breakdown for "The Spiral Protocol," detailing the Infrastructure-Punk sensory anchors, Succession-style subtextual power struggles, and Bio-Resonance technical status.

## Act I: The Fault Line
**Goal:** Establish the status quo, the rivalry, and the inciting incident that forces the descent.

### Scene 1: The Gilded Cage
*   **Location:** Upper Deck Observation Lounge.
*   **Sensory Anchor:** Scent of Santal 33, expensive silk, star-speckled void.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Kyle performs high-status boredom to dismiss Board panic.
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Baseline (Green).

### Scene 2: The Metabolic Scream
*   **Location:** Upper Deck / Observation Lounge.
*   **Sensory Anchor:** The tectonic roar of the station's core; the shattering of a scotch glass from sheer frequency.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Kyle realizes his mask is useless against the station's collapse.
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Critical Spike (Amber-Red).

### Scene 3: The Descent Begins
*   **Location:** The Sector 4 Service Lift.
*   **Sensory Anchor:** The transition from pressurized silence to the clatter of industrial machinery.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Kyle's internal struggle with his "Shuck" origins as he moves toward the mud.
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Unstable.

### Scene 4: The Sump and the Sovereign
*   **Location:** Maintenance Sector 4 (The Gut-Works).
*   **Sensory Anchor:** Black hydraulic slurry, 14kHz rejection whine, Andrew's sweat-slicked skin.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Andrew uses "Corporate Vulgarianism" to mock Kyle's pristine presence.
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Localized Failure.

### Scene 5: The Bypass Blowout
*   **Location:** Cooling Bypass 4-Alpha.
*   **Sensory Anchor:** Scalding cooling gel fog (sweet/chemical), the physical heat of the steam.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Kyle proves his technical mastery to a skeptical Andrew; "Competence-Lust" begins.
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Manual Stabilization (1st Sync).

### Scene 6: The Liaison's Mandate
*   **Location:** Sector 4 Hub.
*   **Sensory Anchor:** Sarah Lin's clinical, low-res hologram; the "clunk" of the titanium bulkheads sealing.
*   **Subtextual Goal:** Sarah exerts dominance by framing their survival as a "Hostile Merger."
*   **Bio-Resonance Status:** Protocol Active (Lockdown).
