To satisfy the 2026 "Tech-Savvy Niche Enthusiast," the system must provide more than a simple link; it must act as a verifiable certificate of ownership and a portal to the "Aether-Network."

### **A. Hardware Requirements**
*   **NFC Chip Type:** **NTAG424 DNA** (Secure NFC).
    *   **Reasoning:** Unlike standard NTAG213 chips, the 424 DNA supports **AES-128 encryption** and **SUN (Secure Unique NFC) message generation**. This prevents URL cloning and ensures that each "Provenance" tap is unique to that specific physical book.
*   **Placement:** Embedded within the **back-cover board** (underneath the Obsidian Suede material) or integrated into a **metallic bookplate**.
*   **Shielding:** High-quality ferrite foil backing to ensure the chip remains readable even when placed near metallic bookshelves or magnetic surfaces.

### **B. Software & Verification Logic**
*   **Provenance Protocol:** Upon tap, the chip generates a unique, one-time encrypted URL.
*   **Verification Portal:** A custom, mobile-optimized landing page styled as the **"Imperial Archive Interface."**
*   **Blockchain Integration (Optional but recommended for 2026):** Each physical book is mapped to a digital twin (NFT or similar ledger entry) to verify the "First Press" number and ownership history.
*   **UX Flow:** 
    1.  User taps phone to the back cover.
    2.  Browser opens to a secure authentication sequence (Visual: Aether-veins pulsing on screen).
    3.  System verifies the chip's unique key.
    4.  Access granted to the **"Aether-Core Data Stream."**
