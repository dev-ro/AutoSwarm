# 02_OUTLINE: AETHERFALL (Obsidian & Gold Romantasy)

**Project Code:** Aetherfall  
**Format:** Serialized Novel (Optimized for Kindle Unlimited/Subscription Models)  
**Total Target Word Count:** 85,000 - 95,000 words  
**Core Tropes:** Enemies-to-Lovers, Touch Her and You Die, God-Machine Catalyst, Found Family, Forced Proximity.

---

## **I. ACT 1: THE SPARK & THE TRIAL (0% – 25%)**
*Theme: Captivity, Forced Proximity, Discovery.*

*   **Chapter 1: The Cinder-Girl.** Introduce Caelia Thorne. She’s working a dead-end job in the obsidian mines, hiding her aether-sensitivity. World-building: The decay of Aethelgard.
*   **Chapter 2: The Shadow Commander.** Introduce Kaelen Vance. He arrives in the sector hunting for "unregistered catalysts." He is cold, efficient, and lethally handsome.
*   **Chapter 3: The Extraction.** Kaelen’s units sweep the mines. Caelia’s powers flare during an accident. Kaelen captures her personally. The first spark of "Lethal Tension."
*   **Chapter 4: The Citadel of Glass.** Caelia is taken to the capital. She realizes she’s not being executed—she’s being tested.
*   **Chapter 5: [SERIALITY BEAT: THE 15% HOOK – THE BLOOD TRIAL]** 
    *   Caelia is forced into the "Aether-Synchrony Pit." She must survive a simulated combat trial against a mechanical beast. Kaelen watches from the balcony. 
    *   **The Hook:** Caelia doesn't just survive; she accidentally "rewrites" the machine’s code with her blood. Kaelen realizes she is the "Apex Catalyst" he’s been searching for.
*   **Chapter 6: The Golden Cage.** Caelia is assigned to Kaelen’s personal unit. They are bound by a "Geas-Link"—a magical tether that prevents her from escaping.
*   **Chapter 7: Training & Tension.** Kaelen trains Caelia. High-tension sparring. They hate each other, but the physical proximity is electric.
*   **Chapter 8: The Shadow Court.** Introduction of political rivals. Caelia sees Kaelen’s vulnerability—his struggle with the High Council.

---

## **II. ACT 2: THE FORCED ALLIANCE (25% – 50%)**
*Theme: Secrets, Simmering Heat, Shared Danger.*

*   **Chapter 9: The First Mission.** They are sent to retrieve a lost Aether-Core.
*   **Chapter 10: The "One Bed" Protocol.** During a storm, they are forced to shelter in a derelict God-Machine bunker. Only one heating unit works.
*   **Chapter 11: Vulnerability.** Caelia learns about Kaelen’s past (The Fall of the Fifth Legion). He sees her scars—not just physical ones.
*   **Chapter 12: The Ambush.** A group of "Hollowed" (tech-zombies) attacks. 
*   **Chapter 13: [TROPE BEAT: TOUCH HER AND YOU DIE]** 
    *   Caelia is pinned down. Kaelen goes into a "Primal Rage," decimating the enemies to save her. The dynamic shifts from "Prisoner" to "Partner."
*   **Chapter 14: The Return.** They bring back the Core. The High Council is suspicious of their growing bond.
*   **Chapter 15: The Masquerade.** A gala at the Obsidian Palace. They must pose as a couple to spy on a traitor.
*   **Chapter 16: The Dance.** Intense romantic tension. A near-kiss interrupted by a discovery.
*   **Chapter 17: The Discovery.** Caelia finds evidence that the God-Machines aren't just tools—they are fueled by the souls of catalysts.
*   **Chapter 18: [SERIALITY BEAT: THE 50% SHIFT – THE GREAT BETRAYAL]**
    *   Kaelen’s second-in-command, Lyra, betrays them to the High Council. They are accused of treason. 
    *   **The Twist:** The High Council orders Kaelen to "harvest" Caelia to power the main defense system. Kaelen refuses, choosing her over his duty. They become fugitives.

---

## **III. ACT 3: THE DESCENT (50% – 75%)**
*Theme: Survival, Found Family, Rebellion.*

*   **Chapter 19: The Escape.** They flee into the "Dead Zones" outside the obsidian walls.
*   **Chapter 20: The Resistance.** They find a hidden community of "Burned-out" catalysts. Found Family vibes.
*   **Chapter 21: The Bond Deepens.** Without the Geas-Link, they choose to stay together. The first high-heat scene.
*   **Chapter 22: The God-Machine’s Voice.** Caelia starts hearing the "Spirit" of the ancient God-Machines. They aren't dead; they’re waiting for her.
*   **Chapter 23: Training the Resistance.** Kaelen teaches the refugees to fight. Caelia teaches them to harness their aether without burning out.
*   **Chapter 24: The Pursuit.** The High Council’s "Hunter-Killers" find their location.
*   **Chapter 25: The Battle of the Wastes.** A desperate defense. Caelia successfully pilots a derelict machine to save the camp.
*   **Chapter 26: The Truth of the Core.** They realize Kaelen is also a catalyst—the "Key" to her "Lock." They are Fated Mates in a technological sense.

---

## **IV. ACT 4: THE FINAL SYNCHRONY (75% – 100%)**
*Theme: Sacrifice, Transformation, The Cliffhanger.*

*   **Chapter 27: The March on the Capital.** The Resistance moves to stop the High Council from activating the "World-Eater" protocol.
*   **Chapter 28: Inside the Obsidian Spire.** Stealth and combat. Kaelen and Caelia are a seamless fighting duo.
*   **Chapter 29: The Confrontation.** They face the High Prelate.
*   **Chapter 30: The Trap.** The High Prelate reveals that the "World-Eater" requires *two* sacrifices: The Pilot and the Battery.
*   **Chapter 31: The Duel.** Kaelen fights the High Prelate’s mechanical champion.
*   **Chapter 32: The Activation.** The system begins to overload. The city starts to crumble.
*   **Chapter 33: [SERIALITY BEAT: THE 90% CLIFFHANGER – THE DEADLY CHOICE]**
    *   The God-Machine requires a final "Core-Purge" to prevent the world’s destruction. 
    *   **The Choice:** To save the city, one must stay behind in the reactor. Kaelen shoves Caelia into the escape pod and locks the door. 
    *   **The Cliffhanger:** The spire explodes in a flash of gold and violet. Caelia screams as her pod is ejected. The book ends with her landing in the ruins, her aether-bond with Kaelen going dead... but then, a faint, rhythmic mechanical heartbeat pulses in her mind.
*   **Chapter 34: Epilogue (The Hook for Book 2).** Caelia stands in the ashes. Her eyes have turned from Amber to Obsidian. She looks at the wreckage of the Spire. "I'm coming for you."
