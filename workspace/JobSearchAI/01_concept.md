# Project Concept: JobSearchAI - GenAI Job Report

## Objective
To identify and compile a high-value list of "Senior Python freelance/contract remote" job opportunities that specifically feature "Generative AI" requirements as of early February 2026.

## Target Audience
Senior Python Developers looking for freelance or contract work with a focus on cutting-edge AI technologies.

## Key Deliverables
- A structured report of top job listings.
- Application links and specific requirements (keywords).
- Brief market trend analysis for the GenAI sector.
