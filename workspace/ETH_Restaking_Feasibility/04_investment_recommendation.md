## 2. The Risk-Mismatch Analysis
Pure restaking (moving 100% of 5 ETH into protocols like EigenLayer) introduces several "additive" risks that exceed your comfort level:

*   **Multi-Layer Slashing**: Unlike standard staking where you only face Ethereum protocol penalties, restaking exposes you to penalties from Actively Validated Services (AVSs), such as oracles or data bridges.
*   **Smart Contract Stacking**: Investing in a Liquid Restaking Token (LRT) involves trusting four distinct layers of code: Ethereum Core → Liquid Staking Code → EigenLayer Contracts → LRT Provider Contracts.
*   **Liquidity Constraints**: Restaking protocols often have longer withdrawal lead times or variable liquidity compared to established liquid staking tokens (LSTs).
